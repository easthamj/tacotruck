Brothers Juan and Carlos Rodriguez grew up in a small town in Mexico, learning how to cook from their abuela, Maria. She taught them the secrets of making the most delicious tacos, using fresh ingredients and a variety of spices and seasonings.

Years later, the brothers decided to leave their hometown and bring their abuela's recipes to the United States. They started a food truck business, serving up their signature tacos and other Mexican street foods to hungry customers around the city.

Today, Juan and Carlos are proud to continue their abuela's legacy and share her love of cooking with others. They are dedicated to using only the freshest ingredients and authentic recipes, ensuring that each bite of their food is a taste of Mexico.

Visit Tacos Primeros Food Truck to taste the love and passion that Juan and Carlos have for their food and culture.
